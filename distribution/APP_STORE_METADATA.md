## Description (English)

```
ChildTime turns your child's screen time into a learning game.

Your child picks a world, answers math or Hebrew questions, and
earns minutes to play in the apps you approved — YouTube, games,
whatever you decide. They learn without being nagged, and they
have a real reason to do one more.

🎮 How it works
• You pick which apps are blocked until your child earns time
• Your child answers questions in the journey and earns minutes
• The more they succeed — the more stars they collect, the more
  worlds they unlock
• Tofi, a magical friend, guides them and reacts to every win

🏰 Three worlds in the first release
• Numbers Kingdom — addition and subtraction
• Letter Tower — Hebrew spelling
• Dinosaur Valley — multiplication and division

✨ What makes it special
• Difficulty that adapts automatically
• Zero frustration — no red, no "wrong"
• Surprise chests, combo streaks, mystery portals
• Zero ads, zero in-app purchases
• All data stays on device — nothing sent anywhere

🔒 Safety
• Parent settings are PIN-protected
• Uses Apple's Screen Time for real shielding
• Suitable for ages 4-10
```
